Unseen Servant is a basic Order Illusion Summon Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates an invisible, mindless, shapeless force to perform simple tasks. (Tier 1)
Uproot is a basic Chaos Earth Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and violently pulls a creature out of the ground or from deep-rooted earth. (Tier 2)
