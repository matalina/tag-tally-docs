Tasha's Hideous Laughter is a basic Chaos Sound Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and forces one creature into a fit of incapacitating laughter. (Tier 2)
Telekinesis is a complex Chaos Space Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and allows the caster to mentally move creatures or objects up to 1,000 lbs. (Tier 3)
Telepathic Bond is a complex Order Mind Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a permanent, ongoing telepathic link between up to eight creatures. (Tier 2)
Teleport is a complex Chaos Space Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and instantaneously moves up to eight willing creatures to a new location on the same plane. (Tier 3)
Teleportation Circle is a complex Order Space Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a permanent, reusable transport circle between two locations. (Tier 3)
Thorn Whip is a basic Chaos Life Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a thorny vine that deals damage and pulls the target towards the caster. (Tier 2)
Time Stop is a complex Chaos Time Control Manifestation that targets LEVEL 1 things and stops time for everyone but the caster for one turn. (Tier 3)
Tongues is a basic Order Sound Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and allows one creature to understand and speak any language. (Tier 2)
True Polymorph is a complex Chaos Life Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and transforms a creature or object into a completely different creature or object, possibly permanently. (Tier 3)
True Resurrection is a complex Order Death Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and restores a creature to life with all its body parts, even if the body is completely missing. (Tier 2)
True Seeing is a basic Order Light Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants one creature the ability to see through normal and magical darkness, and perceive things as they truly are. (Tier 2)
