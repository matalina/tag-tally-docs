Earthquake is a complex Chaos Earth Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a massive tremor that damages structures and disrupts movement. (Tier 3)
Eldritch Blast is a basic Chaos Void Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and fires beams of crackling energy. (Tier 2)
Elementalism is a basic Chaos Fire Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and performs minor manipulation of a chosen element. (Tier 2)
Enhance Ability is a basic Order Mind Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and improves one of a creature's ability scores. (Tier 2)
Enlarge/Reduce is a basic Order Space Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and changes the size of a creature or object. (Tier 2)
Ensnaring Strike is a basic Chaos Life Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and causes vines to sprout and restrain a hit creature. (Tier 3)
Entangle is a basic Chaos Life Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and causes plants to spring up and restrain creatures in an area. (Tier 3)
Enthrall is a basic Chaos Mind Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and draws the attention of multiple creatures with captivating talk or music. (Tier 3)
Etherealness is a complex Order Space Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and shifts the caster to the Border Ethereal Plane. (Tier 2)
Expeditious Retreat is a basic Order Air Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants the caster the ability to Dash as a bonus action. (Tier 1)
Eyebite is a complex Chaos Mind Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and afflicts up to three creatures with fear, sleep, or sickness by looking at them. (Tier 3)
