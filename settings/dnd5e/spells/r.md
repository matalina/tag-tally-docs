Raise Dead is a complex Order Death Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and restores a dead creature to life, provided it hasn't been dead too long. (Tier 2)
Rage is a basic Chaos Mind Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and forces a creature to become dominated by rage, attacking the nearest creature. (Tier 2)
Ray of Enfeeblement is a basic Chaos Death Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and blasts a creature with a necrotic ray that halves its weapon damage output. (Tier 3)
Ray of Frost is a basic Chaos Water Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and blasts a creature with a frigid ray that deals cold damage and slows their speed. (Tier 2)
Reincarnate is a complex Chaos Life Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and brings a dead creature back to life in a randomly determined new body. (Tier 2)
Remove Curse is a basic Order Void Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and ends one curse affecting a creature or object. (Tier 2)
Resistance is a basic Order Void Ward Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants one creature a minor bonus to one saving throw. (Tier 2)
Resurrection is a complex Order Death Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and restores a creature to life even after a long time, but requires major sacrifices. (Tier 2)
Reverse Gravity is a complex Chaos Space Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and reverses gravity in a large cylinder, causing creatures and objects to fall upward. (Tier 3)
Revivify is a basic Order Death Control Incantation that targets LEVEL 1 things and restores a creature to life instantly if done within one minute of death. (Tier 2)
Rope Trick is a basic Order Space Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a climbable rope that leads to a small, isolated extradimensional space. (Tier 2)
