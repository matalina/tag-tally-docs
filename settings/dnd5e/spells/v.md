Vampiric Touch is a basic Chaos Death Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and deals necrotic damage on a successful attack, healing the caster for half the damage dealt. (Tier 2)
Vicious Mockery is a basic Chaos Sound Damage Incantation that targets LEVEL 1 things and deals psychic damage with an insult, giving the target disadvantage on its next attack. (Tier 2)
Vision is a complex Order Time Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants glimpses of the future or past events through prophetic dreams or visions. (Tier 2)
Vitriolic Sphere is a complex Chaos Water Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and hurls a sphere of caustic acid that deals damage immediately and over time. (Tier 3)
Volley is a basic Order Air Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and fires a massive barrage of projectiles into an area, dealing ranged weapon damage (Approximation). (Tier 2)
Vortex is a complex Chaos Air Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and summons a dangerous, swirling whirlwind that lifts and batters creatures caught inside. (Tier 3)
