Pass Without Trace is a basic Order Shadow Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and gives up to ten creatures a bonus to Stealth checks. (Tier 3)
Passwall is a complex Order Earth Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a temporary 10-foot long, 10-foot deep passage through wood, plaster, or stone. (Tier 1)
Phantasmal Killer is a complex Chaos Illusion Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and causes one creature to perceive their worst fear as reality, dealing psychic damage. (Tier 2)
Phantom Steed is a basic Order Illusion Summon Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates an illusionary, but tangible, horse that can move quickly. (Tier 1)
Planar Binding is a complex Order Spirit Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and binds an elemental or outsider to serve you for a period of time. (Tier 1)
Plane Shift is a complex Chaos Space Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and sends up to eight creatures to a different plane of existence. (Tier 3)
Plant Growth is a basic Chaos Life Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and causes plants to grow instantly, either enriching soil or hindering movement. (Tier 3)
Poison Spray is a basic Chaos Death Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and forces a creature to make a Constitution save or take poison damage. (Tier 2)
Polymorph is a complex Chaos Life Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and transforms a creature into a different creature, changing their physical form and statistics. (Tier 3)
Power Word Kill is a complex Chaos Death Damage Incantation that targets LEVEL 1 things and instantly slays one creature with 100 hit points or fewer using a single word. (Tier 2)
Power Word Stun is a complex Chaos Mind Control Incantation that targets LEVEL 1 things and instantly stuns one creature with 150 hit points or fewer using a single word. (Tier 2)
Prayer of Healing is a basic Order Life Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and heals up to six creatures in a burst after a brief prayer. (Tier 2)
Precognition is a complex Order Time Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and gives the caster insight into the future, granting advantage on saving throws and attack rolls. (Tier 2)
Preservation is a basic Order Life Ward Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and extends the life of an organism, preventing sickness and aging. (Tier 2)
Prismatic Spray is a complex Chaos Light Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and fires a cone of seven different colored beams, each with a unique effect. (Tier 3)
Private Sanctum is a complex Order Void Ward Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and wards a large area against scrying, teleportation, and sound. (Tier 2)
Produce Flame is a basic Chaos Fire Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a handheld flame that can be thrown as a ranged attack. (Tier 2)
Programmed Illusion is a complex Order Illusion Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a complex illusion that triggers when certain conditions are met. (Tier 1)
Project Image is a complex Chaos Illusion Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a perfectly realistic illusionary duplicate of the caster that can move and speak. (Tier 2)
Protection from Energy is a basic Order Void Ward Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants a creature resistance to a chosen damage type (Fire, Cold, Acid, etc.). (Tier 2)
Protection from Evil and Good is a basic Order Light Ward Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and protects one creature against certain types of celestial, fiend, or undead creatures. (Tier 1)
Purify Food and Drink is a basic Order Life Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and removes poison and disease from up to 10 cubic feet of material. (Tier 3)
