Wall of Fire is a complex Chaos Fire Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a sheer wall of fire that deals damage upon entry and exit. (Tier 3)
Wall of Force is a complex Order Void Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates an invisible, impenetrable barrier of magical force. (Tier 3)
Wall of Ice is a complex Chaos Water Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a massive, cold wall that can damage creatures that pass through it. (Tier 3)
Wall of Stone is a complex Order Earth Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a non-magical, permanent wall of solid rock. (Tier 2)
Wall of Thorns is a complex Chaos Life Summon Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a thick barrier of giant, razor-sharp thorns and heavy undergrowth. (Tier 3)
Warding Bond is a basic Order Void Ward Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a magical link where damage taken by one creature is split with the caster. (Tier 2)
Water Breathing is a basic Order Water Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants up to ten willing creatures the ability to breathe underwater. (Tier 2)
Water Walk is a basic Order Water Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and grants up to ten willing creatures the ability to walk across the surface of water. (Tier 2)
Web is a basic Chaos Life Control Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and creates a large area of thick, sticky, flammable webs that restrain creatures. (Tier 3)
Wish is a complex Chaos Time Control Manifestation that targets LEVEL 1 things and replicates the effect of any other spell or creates nearly any outcome. (Tier 3)
Witch Bolt is a basic Chaos Light Damage Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and fires a blast of crackling energy that deals damage turn after turn. (Tier 2)
Word of Recall is a complex Order Space Control Incantation that targets LEVEL 1 things and teleports the caster and up to five allies instantly to a previously designated sanctuary. (Tier 2)
