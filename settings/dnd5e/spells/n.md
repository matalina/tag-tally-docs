Nondetection is a basic Order Void Ward Somatic that targets LEVEL 1 things and prevents a creature or object from being targeted by divination or sensing magic. (Tier 2)
Nystul's Magic Aura is a basic Order Illusion Control Ritual that targets LEVEL 1 things and alters or masks the magical aura of an object or creature. (Tier 1)
