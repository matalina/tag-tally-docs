> [!abstract] Type
> **Name** is a \[descriptor\] \[type\] who \[specializes in\]

For each DnD 5e conversion pick a descriptor and a specializes in variant that best describes your character.

## Barbarian is a [descriptor] [warrior] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Savage, Fierce, Primal, Untamed

**Specializes in:**
* overwhelms enemies with frenzied attacks
* fights with primal instinct
* draws strength from nature
* channels divine fury

* (flaw) Hot-headed (wrath)
* Rage
* Reckless Attack

## Bard is a [descriptor] [performer] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Charismatic, Inspiring, Wily, Enigmatic

**Specializes in**
* enthralls audiences with mesmerizing performances
* bends hearts and minds with captivating charm
* wields knowledge and magic to outwit any challenge
* inspires allies while excelling in battle

* (flaw) Easily distracted (?)
* Inspiring
* Can Use Illusion, Space, Mind, Shadow and Light Magic

## Cleric is a [descriptor] [priest] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Devout, Pious, Protective, Zealous

**Specializes in**
* heals and protects with divine power
* radiates brilliance to banish darkness
* deceives and manipulates
* strikes fearlessly on the battlefield with martial might

* (flaw) Judgemental (pride)
* Can use Light and Life Magic
* Turn Undead

## Druid is a [descriptor] [guardian] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Natural, Wild, Harmonious, Mystical

**Specializes In**
* harnesses the natural world
* transforms into powerful beasts
* commands the waves and creatures of the ocean
* draws guidance and power from the constellations

* (flaw) Reclusive (pride)
* Has an Animal Form
* Can Use Life, Light, and Elemental magics

## Fighter is a [descriptor] [soldier] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Skilled, Formidable, Tactical, Resolute

**Specializes In**
* controls the battlefield
* excels in raw strength
* blends martial skill with arcane spellcasting
* channels psychic energy

* (flaw) Overconfident (pride)
* Weapons Master
* Has a Fighting Style (sword/shield, two-handed, archery, etc)

## Monk is a [descriptor] [Ascetic] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Disciplined, Focused, Agile, Enlightened

**Specializes In**
* heals allies and strikes foes with precision
* moves unseen and strikes from darkness
* manipulates elemental forces
* masters martial arts

* (flaw) Stoic (pride)
* Flurry of Blows
* Inner Energy

## Paladin is a [descriptor] [champion] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Righteous, Noble, Valiant, Just

**Specializes In**
* embodies honor, courage, and righteous justice
* seeks heroic deeds and legendary renown
* protects life and the natural order
* punishes the guilty

* (flaw) Rigid Morality (pride)
* Can use Life and Light Magic
* Aura of Protection

## Ranger is a [descriptor] [scout] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Tracker, Stealthy, Resourceful, Wilderness

**Specializes In**
* fights alongside a devoted animal companion
* moves between worlds and wields fey magic
* strikes unseen from the shadow
* tracks and eliminates any threat with skill and precision

* (flaw)  Lone-wolf (pride)
* Natural Explorer
* Marksman

## Rogue is a [descriptor] [thief] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Cunning, Agile, Deceptive, Shadowy

**Specializes In**
* blends magic and cunning to outsmart foes
* strikes lethally and with deadly precision
* manifests psychic blades to pierce enemies
* excels at stealth, agility, and clever tricks

* (flaw) self-serving (envy)
* Sneak Attack
* Evasive

## Sorcerer is a [descriptor] [Spellcaster] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Magical, Gifted, Elemental, Innate

**Specializes In**
* wields chaotic magic from beyond reality
* commands precise, orderly arcane forces
* channels the power of dragons
* unleashes unpredictable bursts of magical energy

* (flaw) Impulsive
* Can use any Magic
* Born with the Gift

## Warlock is a [descriptor] [fighter] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Pactbound, Mystical, Ambitious, Occult

**Specializes In**
* bargained with fey powers
* called on radiant power
* draws infernal power
* channels alien knowledge and psychic influence

* (flaw) Obsessive (pride)

## Wizard is a [descriptor] [mage] who [specializes in].

**Descriptor:** Learned, Scholarly, Arcane, Wise

**Specializes In**
* protects and wards against harm

* deceives the senses

* (flaw) arrogant
